Bio

I am presently serving as a Lecturer in the Department of Printing Technology, Regional Institute of Printing Technology Kolkata since July 2017. I was previously associated with DIC India Ltd as a Technical Services Executive (Offset Division) for three years (2014-2017) before joining my present organization. I hold a Bachelor in Printing Engineering degree from Jadavpur University, Kolkata. My teaching areas include digital prepress workflows, color management and print standardization. My research involves developing cost effective alternatives to print metrological devices leveraging computational intelligence. Curriculum vitae
