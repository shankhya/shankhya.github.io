Bio.

I am presently serving as a Lecturer in the Department of Printing Technology, Regional Institute of Printing Technology Kolkata since July 2017. I was previously associated with DIC India Ltd as a Technical Services Executive (Offset Division) for three years (2014-2017) before joining my present organization. I hold a Bachelor in Printing Engineering degree from Jadavpur University, Kolkata. My broad area of interest includes Pre-Press with emphasis on Implementation of Open Source Color Management System for Desktop Printing Systems and Print Standardization.
