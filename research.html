Research

2022 (Ongoing): Development of open source low cost print metrological devices

  1. The primary aim of the research project was to create an accessible and less expensive method for print density measurement by utilizing a flatbed scanner and advanced regression models. This approach was intended to democratize quality control in printing by providing small and medium-sized print shops with a tool that could substitute for traditional, costly densitometers. The research also aimed to compare the effectiveness of different machine learning algorithms in predicting print density based on scanner data, thereby optimizing the accuracy and reliability of this novel method.
